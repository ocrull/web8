Gymnastics

Like I said earlier I like gymnastics. This will be some information about the US womens team.


At the Paris 2024 games, the USA Gymnastics team won gold in all-around. There was 5 of them on the team. Simone Biles (the goat), Jordan Chiles, Jade Carey, Suni Lee, and Hezly Rivera.

This is the US women's gymnastics team in Paris at the 2024 Summer Olympics

Here's everyone on the team and a little bit about them

      Simone Biles is considered the GOAT in gymnastics and has 5 skills named after her.
      Suni Lee is a 22-year-old gymnast from Minnesota, has earned six Olympic medals and at the 2020 olympic has the what was considered the hardest bars routine.
      Jordan Chiles is 24 and from Oregon. She has earned both a gold and a silver Olympic medal.
      Jade Carey won a Olympic gold medal on floor at the 2020 Tokyo Games and got a bronze medal on vault at the 2024 Olympics
      Hezly Rivera is a 16-year-old gymnast from New Jersey and the youngest member of the US Olympic team.
